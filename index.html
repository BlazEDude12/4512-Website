Introducing FRC Team 4512

OtterChaos Innovate. Educate. Inspire.

We are a student-led robotics team combining engineering, creativity, and a whole lot of otter energy. We design, build, and program competition robots — and we have a blast doing it.

4512
Who We Are

Chaos Is Our
Superpower

OtterChaos is FRC Team 4512 — a passionate group of students, mentors, and community members who believe that robots are cool, but the people who build them are even cooler.

Founded with a love of tinkering and a shameless otter obsession, we compete in FIRST Robotics Competition each season. Every year we face a brand-new challenge: six weeks to design, build, wire, and program a 125-pound robot. The chaos is real. The fun is realer.

Beyond the robot, we're a community. We teach each other, lift each other up, and prove that collaboration beats competition — on and off the field.
